BKL: Barely Keeping Life

From study group to chaotic survival squad

Our Downhill Journey

2023 - The Beginning

At starting 10-15 students helping each other with notes and assignments. The golden era of productivity in group.

Mid 2023 - The Great Split

Members dropped to 8-9. People began forming their own groups. The first signs of chaos emerged.

Late 2023 - The Rebranding

Officially became BKL: Barely Keeping Life. frountbanch -------> backbanch to backchodi, masti, and endless randirona.

2024 - The Final Six

The squad solidified: 6 members united by chaos, each bringing unique (questionable) skills to the group.

The Chaotic Core

The avoidance

Expert in last-minute miracles

Special Skills:

Can finish 1-week assignment in 3 hours. Master of "aaj kare soo, kal kara, kal kare soo parso".

Weakness:

Actual deadlines (they're merely suggestions)

The Meme Lord

Chief Entertainment Officer

Special Skills:

Finds relatable memes for every situation. Can turn any serious moment into comedy.

Weakness:

Actually studying instead of making memes about not studying

The Ghost

Master of Disappearance

Special Skills:

Can vanish during exam prep. Reappears only when notes are complete.

Weakness:

Actually showing up when needed

The Overthinker

Professional Crisis Creator

Special Skills:

Can imagine worst-case scenarios for simplest tasks. Turns "submit assignment" into existential crisis.

Weakness:

Basic life decisions

The Parent

Group's Responsible Adult

Special Skills:

Reminds everyone about deadlines. Actually reads syllabus. Brings snacks.

Weakness:

Carrying the mental load of 5 other people

The Wildcard

Unpredictable Element

Special Skills:

Randomly disappears for days. Returns with bizarre stories. Somehow passes everything.

Weakness:

Basic human routine

Inside Jokes That Keep Us Going

  • "Remember that time we actually studied? Me neither."
  • "We'll start at 5" (never starts before 7)
  • The legendary "one last meme" before study that lasts 3 hours
  • "I'll just quickly check my phone" → 2am Wikipedia rabbit hole
  • The shared Google Doc that's 90% nonsense, 10% actual notes
  • "This exam will be easy" → *collective failure*

How Long Will This Last?

Honestly? No one knows. We're just here for the ride, the memes, and the occasional academic crisis.
